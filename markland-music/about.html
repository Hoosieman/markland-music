Cole Markland's musical journey showcases his constant evolution.

Cole is a passionate musician whose love for music has been a driving force in his life since a young age. His ability to play music by ear sets him apart, allowing him to express himself through a wide array of instruments, including guitar, piano, and trumpet. He believes that music is a universal language, capable of bridging gaps and healing the deepest of pains. Whether performing or composing, Cole’s dedication to his craft reflects his understanding that music holds a unique power to connect, inspire, and uplift.

 

Cole Markland's composition Melting Storm draws deeply from an intense moment in his life, one marked by a dramatic and intense storm in the streets of Villa Del Plata, a small town in Argentina. At the time, Markland was serving as a religious missionary, and during this storm, the streets were transformed into raging rivers, rising to waist-high depths. Struggling to cross these torrential waters to return home, Markland found himself in the midst of both physical and emotional turmoil, grappling with the overwhelming forces of nature and his own inner conflict. This raw experience would become the heart of Melting Storm, a composition that blends atmospheric intensity with the stark contrast of calm reflection. Throughout the piece's creation, Markland often revisited the emotions he felt during that moment of uncertainty and vulnerability, reflecting on the chaotic force of the storm, but also on the quiet resilience that allowed him to push forward. The composition is structured to evoke the transition between turmoil and tranquility, symbolizing the confrontation between external chaos and the internal stillness required to overcome it. As Markland worked through the piece, he found himself translating the storm’s surging energy into powerful, dynamic rhythms, and the eerie calm of the aftermath into soft, contemplative melodies. The piece captures both the ferocity of the natural world and the introspective space he found in that moment of peril. Melting Storm thus stands as a musical meditation on the tension between external challenges and the quiet, often hidden strength we summon within to navigate them. It reflects Markland's journey not only through the storm itself but through the deeper layers of self-awareness and spiritual growth that followed.
